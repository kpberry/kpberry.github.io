  • Physics Engine
  • Efficiently calculates forces and interactions at arbitrary distances
  • Forces can be set up easily based on arbitrary rules by following a Force template
  • Includes variable Gravitational, Electrostatic, Spring, Resistive Fluid Forces
  • Includes, as an example, the "Love" Force, which increases in magnitude as the square of the displacement between two particles and is, ostensibly, the strongest force of all

  • Simulations obey conservation of energy, mass, and momentum (when there are no external forces)
